## internet

 - [philosopher.life](https://philosopher.life) - warning, long page load; a
   log of life of a person with an interesting writing style (meant positively!)
   and a lot of content
 - systemspace[^1] was an experience that i certainly have not forgot. (if
   you're a former migrant, come say hi!)
 - [corru.network](https://corru.network/). if you enjoy
   [fauux](https://fauux.neocities.org) (epilepsy/sound warning),
   [angusnicneven](https://angusnicneven.com), or systemspace, you'll enjoy this
   as well
 - [drew devault's blog](https://drewdevault.com/) is a bit less artsy than the
   last few links, but certainly makes good reading
 - [cyberia](https://cyberia.club/), which i am ostensibly a member of, and who
   are very cool people
 - for more internet diving, check out [peelopaalu](https://peelopaalu.neocities.org),
   with a warning that their sensibilities are less easily offended than mine

## media

### anime

those who know me well know i cannot sit through a series; the fact that these
appear here is a testament to how well they can grip you. mal links given largely
so that i have a link to give - in a world without terms of service i'd give
you a link to watch them through

 - [serial experiments lain](https://myanimelist.net/anime/339/Serial_Experiments_Lain),
   human connection, isolation, and the then-burgeoning internet
 - [sonny boy](https://myanimelist.net/anime/48849/Sonny_Boy), coming-of-age done
   strikingly well; frequently throws entire curveballs at your expectations
 - [perfect blue](https://myanimelist.net/anime/437/Perfect_Blue) is not perfect,
   but certainly comes close in its mind-twisting plot

### music

 - [gregory and the hawk](https://gregoryandthehawk.com/album/moenie-and-kitchi),
   who has been the soundtrack to all of the life that i can remember
 - [crywank](https://crywank.bandcamp.com/album/tomorrow-is-nearly-yesterday-and-everyday-is-stupid),
   who similarly has given word and sound to all my simple teenage emotions
 - [starry cat](https://starrycat.bandcamp.com/) and, by extension, any of
   [teen suicide](https://teensuicide.bandcamp.com/album/waste-yrself)'s
   related projects - low fidelity guitar recordings make my brain tingle
 - [etsuko yakushimaru](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a_6quQ994JI) -
   [パラレル、パラレル、パラレル、パラレル、パラレルワード〜]{.unifont}
